About Me

Hi! I’m Nicole, and I am a rising sophomore in Dietrich College of Humanities and Social Sciences at CMU. I’ve always found it fascinating how technology shapes and bring changes to our lives. More specifically, I grew a huge interest in learning how technology and people combine together to create values and solve problems. Currently, I am pursuing a B.S. in Information Systems and an intended additional major in Human-Computer Interactions.

Outside of academics, I have a passion for teaching. Since high school, I have been an instructor and content creator at TechGirlz where I coordinated and taught STEM workshops for middle school girls. Starting this summer, I will also be a teaching assistant for 15-112, Fundamentals of Programming and CS, which includes leading recitations and grading. In my free time, I love baking and cooking with my family, working out (sometimes with my 6-month-old puppy), and jamming to my spotify playlist.
